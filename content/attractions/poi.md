When the train signal sounds, the market transforms as stalls are moved back and umbrellas closed. Once the train passes, the market returns to normal instantly.

It is smaller and much easier to navigate than the Northern (Mo Chit) or Southern (Sai Tai Mai) terminals. Its location right next to the BTS makes it the most accessible bus hub for international tourists.
